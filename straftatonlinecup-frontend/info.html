What is this?

The STRAFTAT Weekly Cup is a fan run tournament for the game STRAFTAT.
Tournaments run weekly, opening for registration at 22:00 UTC on Saturday evening, and starting at 15:00 UTC the following Saturday.

How do I join a tournament?

Just come to the home page of this site, log in with steam, and click register on the open cup. If there is space you will be added to the list.
Then come back at 15:00 UTC on Saturday for the tournament :)

Game Rules

When creating a lobby for a match in a tournament, you must use the following rules:

9 rounds

All default maps

No DLC maps

What do I do if my opponent doesn't show up?

If your opponent doesn't show up within 30 minutes, refresh the match page. You will see a purple button, click it to advance to the next round.
