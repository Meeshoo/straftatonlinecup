STRAFTAT WEEKLY CUP
Match Past Cups FAQ

What is this?

The STRAFTAT Weekly Cup is a fan run tournament for the game STRAFTAT.

What is the tournament format?

A simple single elimination bracket, one loss and you are out.

When does the tournament start?

Tournaments run every Saturday, starting at 15:00 UTC.

How do I play in a tournament?

Simply log in with steam using the button at the top right, and click register on the open cup on the home page. If there is space you will be added to the list.

What do I do if my opponent doesn't show up?

If your opponent doesn't show up within 20 minutes, refresh the match page. You will see a purple button, click it to advance to the next round.
